Photographer Bio

PETER LINDGREN

Photographer

I’m not your ordinary Filmmaker that picked up a camera as a kid, because i didn’t really know how to use a camera until the age of 26. The entire reason i got started with Photography & Videography was because I needed to take some photos for my Fitness Apparel brand that i tried to start up. Since I couldn’t really afford a product photographer, I decided to borrow a camera from a friend, and started learning the basics.
